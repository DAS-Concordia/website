# Sharon Ho

Sharon Ho (she/her) is a MASc student in Software Engineering at Concordia University under the [NSERC CREATE SE4AI](https://se4ai.org/) training program. Her research focuses on the social and ethical implications of synthetic media and generative AI systems. She is co-supervised by Dr. Emad Shihab (DAS Lab) and Dr. Tanja Tajmel ([EDI Lab](https://www.concordia.ca/ginacody/research/edi-lab.html)).
